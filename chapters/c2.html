Shishido Michio is your normal Japanese high school student weeb, living alone in the suburbs of Tokyo. On the fateful day of August 1st, 2008, his collection of anime girl figures and his anime girl body pillow suddenly come to life and become life-sized. Michio's life is completely changed, as his story follows a path of shock, despair, romance, and lust.

Chapter 2

Koyama sighs and gets back to leaning on the wall. Arizumi and Kirarise talking about… how much they love me???

Arizumi giggles. “We love you, Shishido!”
What the fuck???

Koyama leans on the wall with a look of jealousy, as Amasuki is raging over the game she’s playing with Izomi and Kanenako.

“Fucking stupid ass game! Why am I dying to nothing???”

I chuckle a bit to myself. These waifus really are like how I designed them…I can’t believe this is real…

I take another look at Koyoma, who’s leaning on the wall. An idea starts forming in my brain as my neurons activate. Hmm…. how would this play out?

I walk over to her as she starts talking.

“What do you want, Michi-”

DON!

I do a kabedon and lean in close, as she starts blushing. All the waifus are looking at us two.

“W-W-W-what???”

I lean in closer. Our lips are centimeters apart. She blushes harder.

“M-M-M-M-Michio-me… I… I… y-you…”

I mentally prepare myself for what I’m gonna do. Here goes nothing…

“You look so cute when you’re shy, Koyoma…”

She blushes so hard she becomes a tomato.

“EH?!?!??!??!!?”

The 6 other waifus snicker to themselves. Ryotose speaks up.

“I think Michio-me’s right about you being cute, Koyoma~”

SHOOM

I look to see Koyoma has escaped from the kabedon position by sliding, as I turn around to meet her scowling face.

“W-W-What was t-that for, i-i-idiot???”

GULP

I think she’s mad…

GULP

I might’ve fucked up really badly right now…

BA-DUM BA-DUM

Koyoma runs out of the room while screaming.

“Idiooooooooot!”

Ryotose follows her as she mocks her. “Awwww, is the tomato running away?”

BA-DUM BA-DUM

ba-dum ba-dum

My heart calms down. I don’t think my “idea” was a good one…
